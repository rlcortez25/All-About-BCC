Incoming Freshmen

To be admitted, all incoming freshmen should pass the admission test conducted by the Guidance Office and Testing Center.

  • 1. Form 138 or High School Report Card
  • 2. Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • 3. Two (2) copies of 2x2 colored picture
  • 4. PSA Birth Certificate (Photocopy)
  • 5. One (1) Long brown envelope

Transferees

The school admits transferees from other educational institutions provided that they have complied with the requirements and procedure provided above for incoming freshmen and the following additional documentary requirements:

  • 1. Certificate of Transfer Credential
  • 2. Original Scholastic Records from the institution enrolled in
  • 3. PSA Birth Certificate/Marriage Certificate
  • 4. 2x2 Colored Pictures (2)

Cross Enrollees

Cross-Enrollees from other educational institutions for any academic subjects and/or any of the NSTP components are required to submit the following documentary requirements:

  • 1. Cross-Enrollment Permit from the home school duly signed by their Dean
  • 2. Letter addressed to the Registrar requesting that the cross-enrollee be admitted stating therein the reasons for such request
  • 3. One (1) Long Brown Envelope
